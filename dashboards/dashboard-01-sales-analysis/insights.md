# Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Objective
To analyze car sales performance across manufacturers and models.

## Key Insights
- Certain manufacturers dominate overall sales.
- Fuel efficiency impacts yearly sales trends.
- Sales vary significantly across car models.

## Business Value
This dashboard helps identify top-performing manufacturers and understand the relationship between fuel efficiency and sales.
